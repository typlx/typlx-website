Introducing Typlx: The Open-Source, Privacy-First Alternative to Grammarly

May 15, 2026

In an era where every keystroke is increasingly treated as data to be harvested, the tools we use to improve our writing should be our strongest allies, not silent observers. Today, we are proud to introduce Typlx—the first truly open-source, privacy-first grammar checker powered by advanced Large Language Models (LLMs).

Why Typlx?

Most grammar checkers today operate on a simple but invasive premise: you send them your text, and they send you suggestions. But what happens to that text? Often, it's stored, analyzed, and used to train future models. For developers, writers, and privacy-conscious users, this "cloud-first" approach is a non-starter.

Typlx was born from a simple question: Can we have world-class writing assistance without sacrificing our privacy?

The answer is yes.

What Makes Typlx Different?

1. Open-Source to the Core

Typlx isn't just a free tool; it's an open-source movement. Every line of our checking logic, our browser extensions, and our mobile keyboards is available for audit and contribution on GitHub. We believe that the tools that shape our communication should belong to the community.

2. Privacy by Design (Local-First Inference)

We believe your writing should stay yours. Typlx is built for Local-First Inference, giving you Sovereign Data Control. While we offer cloud-based processing for convenience, our goal is to empower users to run Typlx entirely on their own infrastructure or even locally on their machines. No more worrying about your private emails or confidential documents being stored on someone else's server.

3. Contextual Intelligence

Unlike traditional rule-based grammar checkers that struggle with the nuances of language, Typlx leverages the power of LLMs to understand context. It doesn't just look for typos; it understands the meaning of your sentences, offering suggestions that improve tone, clarity, and flow while respecting your unique voice.

4. Built for Developers

We know where developers spend their time. That's why Typlx is designed to work seamlessly in the environments you already use. From README files and documentation to commit messages and GitHub PRs, Typlx helps you communicate as clearly as you code.

The Road Ahead

Today's launch is just the beginning. Our 90-day roadmap includes:

  • Phase 1: Developer Soft Launch (Chrome Extension + GitHub Repository)
  • Phase 2: Public Expansion & SEO Foundation
  • Phase 3: Mobile Keyboards (iOS & Android)

Join the Movement

Typlx is more than just a product; it's a statement that privacy and powerful AI can coexist. We invite you to try our Chrome extension, star our repository on GitHub, and join our growing community on Discord.

Your words. Your privacy. Typlx.
